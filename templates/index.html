About Goreto Treks

Nepal Best Travel Agency Company Since 2018

Goreto Treks is a newly started company, by energetic youths who have excellent experience in the field of tourism industry for almost a decade. Goreto Treks is established with the slogan “Smiling Mountains await you”. We will assist our clients in reaching upto those mountains and to smile back at them. Basically, we organize adventure trekking, hiking, mountain expeditions, city tour/Sightseeing, cultural tours, national park visits, rafting, jungle safaris, bird watching, paragliding, mountain flight, bungy jumping, etc. in Nepal. Beside these activities, we organize city tours in neighboring country India as well. Also Goreto Treks tries its best to make the journey of our clients filled with numerous wonderful memories by providing experienced and professional team members to those (their ) destinations with low price along with quality services. Our clients can reach to us 24/7 by phone contact, viber, WhatsAPP, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Kakao Talk
